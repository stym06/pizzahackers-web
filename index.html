Ideas Proposed

Anyone who is even remotely associated with NIT Jamshedpur can propose idea on which they want to work on.Idea can be for a product: software,hardware, or, for a talk they want to give on any topic.

Ideas Churned

All ideas proposed before friday of any given week are pH tested. In pH test,the existing pH community members vote on the ideas.
Churning out milk,leaves butter.

Products Hacked

Ideas that pass the pH test are put up as target on weekends. Talks are held on Saturdays then incessant hacking till product completion begins.
Real fun part. You break,learn,build,iterate.
